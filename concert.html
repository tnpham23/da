Concert Analytics Dashboard

Excel & Tableau: Pivot Tables & Geospatial Visualization

My most recent project focuses on the revenue analysis of Taylor Swift concerts, one of the most influential artists of our generation. I performed data cleaning within Excel/Google Sheets and created a dashboard within Tableau to support my actionable recommendations to Swift Concert Management that would optimize tour sales and logistic planning. Compiled into Google Slides for reporting, I summarize key insights and implement statistical analysis and visualizations for a final presentation.
